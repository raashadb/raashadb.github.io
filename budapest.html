The Hungarian Parliament Building is the seat of the National Assembly of Hungary. Located on the banks of the Danube, it is one of Europe's oldest legislative buildings and a stunning example of Neo-Gothic architecture.

Highlights include the Crown Jewels, 691 decorated rooms, and breathtaking views of the Danube River.
